What is Risk of Ruin?

Risk of Ruin is the probability that you will lose your entire trading account (or hit a drawdown so severe you can't recover) given your current edge and position sizing.

< 1%
Excellent — your account is very safe. Keep doing what you're doing.
1 – 10%
Caution — reduce position size or improve your win rate / R:R.
> 10%
Danger — you're likely to blow up. Lower risk per trade immediately.

Formula: RoR = ((1 − Edge) ÷ (1 + Edge)) ^ (Account ÷ Risk per trade)

The two biggest levers to reduce ruin are: 1) smaller position sizes, and 2) improving your edge (higher win rate or better R:R).
